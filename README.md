# 📊 Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales data to uncover key business insights such as revenue trends, top-performing products, and regional performance. The goal is to help businesses make data-driven decisions to improve sales and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance  
- Identify top-selling products  
- Understand regional sales distribution  
- Track monthly and yearly sales trends  
- Provide actionable business insights  

---

## 📂 Dataset Features
- Order ID  
- Order Date  
- Customer Name / ID  
- Region  
- Product Category  
- Product Name  
- Quantity  
- Sales Amount  
- Profit  
- Discount  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Highest sales observed in **West region**  
- **Technology category** generates maximum revenue  
- Peak sales during **year-end season (Nov–Dec)**  
- Discounts negatively impact **profit margins**  
- Few products contribute to majority of sales (Pareto principle)  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Total Sales, Profit, Orders)  
- Sales Trend (Monthly/Yearly)  
- Sales by Region  
- Sales by Category  
- Top 10 Products  
- Profit Analysis  
- Discount Impact Analysis  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Excel / CSV  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing regions (West)  
- Increase investment in Technology category  
- Optimize discount strategies to protect profit  
- Promote top-performing products  
- Plan campaigns during peak sales seasons
